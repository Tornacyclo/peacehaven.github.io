Poetic form (prose, haikus,...) or narrative form Le réseau Cap sur le Rhône How to cross the knowledge of the river world with the sensitive, the intimate, the imaginary? How to become aware, through writing, of the importance of the role of rivers, these “paths that walk” and connect us? After addressing the issue of ecological transition (“In the river currents” in 2020-21), the Cap sur le Rhône network is proposing a new edition in 2022-23 around the generic theme “River and mobility”. 31/03/2023 fr
Short Story IUT de Béziers The Fantastic Festival of Béziers is an event open to all, especially to big fans of fantasy. The interest of the fantasy festival is to enhance the image of the IUT of Béziers and the University of Montpellier. The project was born under the impetus of the M.M.I. of the IUT of Béziers and in particular of Mr. Sandy Blanco. This festival will include a competition for short films, short stories and illustrations. An award ceremony (diplomas, trophies and other prizes) will be planned, in order to promote the work produced, but also to motivate more than one to experiment and surpass themselves in their achievements. 31/03/2023 fr
Skit Le Réseau National des Centres de Lutte AntiTuberculeuse The implementation of this first literary challenge follows a study by the "Culture and Tuberculosis" group of the National Network of Anti-Tuberculosis Centers on the place of tuberculosis in the media, carried out in 2021, pointing out the low share attributed to this old pandemic infectious scourge in these, in comparison to the newer coronavirus. 24/03/2023 fr
Poetry La direction des affaires culturelles de la Faculté des Lettres de Sorbonne Université Each year, the Department of Cultural Affairs of the Faculty of Letters of Sorbonne University organizes an international poetry competition. With a free theme, the competition is open to all students enrolled in higher education establishments, in France and abroad, during the current year. 22/03/2023 fr
Short Story Le réseau des Crous The Crous network organizes the New Student Creation competition each year, co-piloted by national level by the Cnous and the Crous of Lille Nord-Pas-de-Calais. 19/03/2023 fr
Short Story Le Dauphiné Libéré On the occasion of L’appel des Sommets, an operation to raise awareness about safeguarding our mountains, Le Dauphiné Libéré is organizing the first edition of a short story competition on the theme “Mountains and Imagination”. The call for papers, open until March 15, 2023, concerns French-speaking works of fiction of a literary nature of a maximum of 10,000 characters (spaces included), without restriction of style or genre. The expected form is that of the short story, namely a short story resolved by a fall. 15/03/2023 fr
Short Story Normandie Livre & Lecture For the 17th edition of its short story competition, Normandie Livre & Lecture, in partnership with the Normandy academic region, invites you to get into sport. Whether you're running, biking, soccer, basketball, rowing, skiing, tennis, high jumping, swimming, or anything else, stay safe. Before putting yourself in the shoes of an athlete, notebook and pen in hand, don't forget to put on a good pair of basketball shoes. 03/03/2023 fr
Poetry Académie des Jeux floraux Poem, in French, encouragement for young poets aged 16 to 25. 16/02/2023 fr
Short Story Les Cahiers du Nem For the year 2022/2023, the Cahiers du Nem are organizing, with the support of the French Development Agency, a short story competition for the journal's website on the theme "Asia and water", under the sponsorship of the novelist and poetess Hoai Huong Aubert-Nguyen. 15/02/2023 fr
Short Story Association Nationale de Formation en Gérontologie (ANFG) The National Association for Training in Gerontology offers a competition for short stories, open to everyone, from 18 years old. The new, unpublished, must include between 8,000 and 18,000 characters (including spaces) and of course respect the characteristics specific to this type of writing (narrative text, outcome or conclusion, etc.). The theme is: Praise of old age. 12/02/2023 fr
